Roxbury Tech Justice Hub

Brand Identity & Visual System

Mission & Message

Tagline: “Building creative power through code, art, and community.”

Our identity celebrates Roxbury’s legacy of resilience and creativity while promoting digital equity. It should feel grounded, warm, and future-focused.

Logo System

Primary mark: Circular emblem with Roxbury Purple base, Gold inner ring, and Teal nodes symbolizing connection.

Wordmark fonts: Montserrat Bold for “Roxbury,” Inter Medium for “Tech Justice Hub.”

Voice & Tone

Keywords: Community, equity, creativity, access, justice, tech, art.

Voice should sound local, human, and action-oriented. Write as if you’re speaking to neighbors — confident but accessible.
